Hello!

My name is Diana Stupino and I am a Web Designer settled in Vancouver B.C.
I am a graduate of Valladolid University in Segovia ( Spain) , with my Bachelor of Advertising and Public Relationship. I am currently working as a web designer at Yellow Pages.
I am a passionate of digital marketing, graphic design, and art. I enjoy learning how to use new digital technologies in order to improve the user experience through the graphic design.

If you want to know more about my work, please have a look to my portfolio.
